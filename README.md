
# Market Analysis Log

## Overview

This repository is a working log of my market research, macro observations, and investment thinking.

The goal is to document how market views form, evolve, and change as new information comes in. Entries range from short observations and signal tracking to longer thesis notes and analytical reports.

This is not meant to be a trading system or formal investment publication. It is a writing-first repository for building clearer, more structured market judgment over time.

## Focus Areas

- Macroeconomic indicators
- Inflation and interest rates
- Credit markets
- Energy markets
- Public equities
- Portfolio risk
- Asset allocation themes
- Market reactions to economic data and policy changes

## Repository Structure


Market-Analysis/
├── notes/      # Short market observations and recurring entries
├── signals/    # Indicator tracking and market signals
├── theses/     # Longer-form views on durable market themes
├── reports/    # More polished analytical writeups
├── data/       # Supporting datasets where relevant
├── scripts/    # Analysis scripts used for charts or calculations
└── README.md


## Guiding Principles


* Consistency matters more than frequency
* Views should change when the facts change
* Short observations are valuable if they are specific
* Writing should clarify thinking, not just summarize headlines
* Data, charts, and models are used when they improve the analysis

## Tooling

* Markdown for written notes and thesis development
* R or Python for data analysis when useful
* Excel for financial modeling and quick market calculations
* Public data sources, filings, macro releases, and research articles where relevant

## Data and Sources

Data sources vary by entry and may include public market data, company filings, macroeconomic releases, central bank materials, earnings reports, and news or research articles.

Sources are linked within individual notes or reports when used.
