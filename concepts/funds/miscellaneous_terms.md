## Misc Fund Structure Terms

### Open-End Fund
- Fund that continuously issues and redeems shares
- Size changes based on investor flows
- Price = NAV

### Closed-End Fund
- Fixed number of shares
- Trades like a stock in the market
- Can trade at premium/discount to NAV

### GP (General Partner)
- Fund manager
- Makes investment decisions and runs the fund

### LP (Limited Partner)
- Investor providing capital
- Passive, no involvement in day-to-day decisions

### Independent Sponsor
- Acts like a PE investor but without a committed fund
- Raises capital deal-by-deal
