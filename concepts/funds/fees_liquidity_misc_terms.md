## Misc Fees & Liquidity Terms

### Incentive Fee / Carried Interest
- Performance-based fee (manager shares in profits)

### Hurdle Rate
- Minimum return required before manager earns incentive fees

### High Watermark
- Manager only earns fees on new profits above previous peak

### Compounding Hurdle
- Benchmark return accumulates over time, not reset yearly

### Lock-Up (Hard vs Soft)
- Period where investors cannot withdraw capital
- Hard = no withdrawals
- Soft = withdrawals allowed with penalty

### Liquidity (Redemption Terms)
- How often investors can withdraw and required notice period

### Gates
- Limits how much capital can be withdrawn at once

### Side Pockets
- Separate bucket for illiquid assets inside a fund
