## Misc Portfolio Management Terms

### NAV (Net Asset Value)
- Value of fund assets minus liabilities
- Determines per-share price

### Gross vs Net IRR
- Gross IRR: return before fees
- Net IRR: return after fees

### Attribution
- Understanding where returns came from (sector, strategy, etc.)

### High-Grading
- Selling weaker assets and reallocating to higher-conviction ideas

### Fund Leverage
- Using borrowed capital to increase exposure

### Restrictions / Limitations
- Constraints on portfolio (e.g., max % in one stock or sector)

### Shorting
- Selling borrowed securities to profit from price declines

### Rebalancing
- Adjusting portfolio weights to maintain target allocation

### Marquee Assets
- High-quality, well-known investments used to signal strength

### Active Management vs Financial Engineering
- Active: value from investment decisions
- Financial engineering: value from structuring/leverage
