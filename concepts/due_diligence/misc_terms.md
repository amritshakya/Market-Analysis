## Misc Due Diligence Terms

### PPM (Private Placement Memorandum)
- Document used by funds to raise capital
- Outlines strategy, risks, structure

### LPA (Limited Partnership Agreement)
- Legal agreement defining GP/LP relationship

### DDQ (Due Diligence Questionnaire)
- Questions investors send to evaluate fund managers

### Operational Due Diligence
- Evaluating non-investment risks (process, controls, infrastructure)

### Data Room
- Secure repository for sharing documents during due diligence
