## Misc Market Structure Terms

### Vertical vs Horizontal Markets
- Vertical: focused on one specific industry (e.g., healthcare software)
- Horizontal: serves many industries (e.g., general cloud tools)
- Useful for thinking about scalability and market size

### Synthetic Index
- Artificially constructed index that mimics market behavior
- Not tied to real assets
- Often used for testing strategies or simulation

### Factor Modeling
- Breaking returns into underlying drivers (e.g., value, growth, momentum)
- Helps explain performance beyond just stock picking
