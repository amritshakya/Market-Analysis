## Misc Macro Terms

### Tailwinds
- Positive external conditions that support growth (e.g., AI adoption, economic expansion, favorable policy)
- Usually used to explain why a sector/company is outperforming

### K-Shaped Economy
- Different parts of the economy recover at different rates after a shock
- Some sectors/people benefit (upper arm), others struggle (lower arm)
- Explains why GDP can look strong while many households feel worse off

### Non-Discretionary Spending
- Spending on essentials (food, rent, utilities)
- Important in inflation environments since these costs can’t easily be cut
